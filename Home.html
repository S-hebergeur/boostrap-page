faq

Look through the answers to the most popular questions from our customers. Didn’t find what you need? Just send us a request and we will get in touch with you shortly.

Do you use materials adapted to your needs?

Yes, we only use materials that are adapted to our needs. Moreover, these materials have the function of repelling the faults that can occur during the daily use of our cars.

When can I expect my order?

All equipment products available for purchase can be delivered upon your request. Special orders will arrive at your door within 6-8 weeks from the time you pay for your order. You can also track the status of your order.

Can I order an individual car model?

Yes. We also provide custom car manufacturing service. Contact us to discuss what item you need, your ideas, design, fabric, and measurements.

What tools species do you use?

We manufacture all car products from tools high-level. We currently use such tools types like wrench, and other hardwoods.

Can the cars be exchanged after their use?

It depends on the type of car. Some products can be exchanged to facilitate the customer and other, others not. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us and tell us all the details.

Do you offer any guarantee?

Yes. We have more than 50 years of experience in the industry and proudly provide our clients with a 1-year guarantee against manufacturing defects on all our car items.
